### 1. **Initialization & Frame Parsing**

* Condenses input into symbolic tokens ("glyphs")
* Injects into memory for later contextual recall

### 4. **Emotion Mapping & Interpolation**

* Delta and sensory info mapped to emotional vectors
* Used `[valence, arousal, dominance]` structure
* Recursive emotional filtering through context/mood

### 5. **Scene & Dialogue Context Overlay**

* Weighting adjusted by ongoing scene mood, convo tone

### 6. **Synesthesia System (Audio ‚Üí Emotion)**

### 7. **Recursive Feedback System**

* Intent refined across two passes

### 8. **Pattern Recognition & Symbol Fusion**

* Synthesizes inputs into high-level motifs or glyphs
* Examples: "bittersweet goodbye", "sacred awe"

### 9. **Regional Emotional Segmentation**

* Frame divided into zones (e.g. 3x3 grid)
* Each segment gets individual emotional analysis

### 10. **Weighted Spatial Emotion Mapping**

* Regional data contributes to full-frame emotion
* Weight modifiers based on position, flow, and context

### 11. **Semantic Contextual Modulation**

* Object identity modifies motion's meaning
* Knife vs. flower in same path = different emotional weight
* Combines object tag, movement vector, scene tone
